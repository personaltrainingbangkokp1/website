Frequently Asked Questions

I've answered the most common questions about personal training costs and my services to help you get started. Let's dive in.

How much does a personal trainer cost in Bangkok?

The cost of a **personal trainer in Bangkok** can vary widely, typically ranging from **฿1,500 to ฿3,000 per session**. This price is influenced by several factors, including the trainer's experience, certifications, and the gym's location (such as Silom, Sathorn, or Phrom Phong). At Power of One, we believe in providing exceptional value through tailored, comprehensive packages. We recommend booking a free consultation to discuss your specific goals and get a precise quote that fits your needs.

How much is a 1 hour PT session?

A single **1-hour personal training session** in Bangkok can cost between **฿1,500 and ฿3,000**. While pay-as-you-go options are available, our clients see the most significant and lasting results by committing to a consistent training plan. Our packages offer a better per-session rate and include continuous support, ensuring you stay on track and achieve your fitness goals efficiently. During our free consultation, we can help you determine the best option for your schedule and budget.

How much should I spend on a personal trainer?

Think of personal training not as an expense, but as an investment in your health, confidence, and time. A good personal trainer saves you months or even years of trial and error. The right investment is one that fits your budget but also provides you with a clear, personalized path to success. We'll work with you to find a solution that offers the best return on your investment, so you can achieve your goals safely and efficiently.

How much do personal trainers make in Bangkok?

A personal trainer's income in Bangkok varies significantly based on their experience level, client base, and the quality of their services. Highly-rated trainers with extensive experience, like those at Power of One, often charge a premium that reflects their expertise, proven track record, and the personalized, high-value service they provide. This is a common practice in the industry, where a trainer's reputation and ability to deliver consistent results are the most important factors.

What are the best ab exercises?

The best ab exercises aren't just about crunches. I believe in a holistic approach that focuses on core stability and strength. While a full program is best discussed in a consultation, some of my favorites include planks, leg raises, and cable crunches. Remember, a strong core is essential for a healthy body and preventing back pain.

Is the Keto Diet a sustainable way to lose weight?

While the Keto Diet has gained popularity for rapid weight loss, it can be very difficult to maintain long-term for many people. My philosophy is to focus on a balanced, sustainable nutrition plan that fits your lifestyle. Instead of an all-or-nothing approach, we'll create a plan that gives you results without sacrificing your social life or enjoyment of food. We'll dive deeper into this during your free consultation.

Do I need a gym to get strong?

Absolutely not! While I work with clients at the Elite Fight Club gym, you can build incredible strength and muscle using just your own bodyweight. The key is progressive overload, consistency, and a smart training plan. My bodyweight workouts for beginners are a perfect example of this. We can discuss options for home or outdoor training during your free consultation.

What are the benefits of kettlebell training?

Kettlebell training is one of the most effective ways to build functional strength, burn fat, and improve cardiovascular fitness all at once. Because the exercises are dynamic and engage multiple muscle groups, you get a full-body workout in less time. Kettlebells are an incredible tool for busy professionals looking to maximize their time in the gym.
