Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from Bangkok clients

Where do you train clients in Bangkok?
I work primarily at Elite Fight Club in Phrom Pong, which is easily accessible by BTS. I also provide training at clients' condos with private gyms and offer comprehensive online training sessions for clients worldwide.
How do you handle Bangkok's heat and pollution during training?
All training is conducted in premium air-conditioned facilities. I design programs that account for Bangkok's climate, focusing on indoor functional movements and proper hydration strategies.
Do you work with expats who don't speak Thai?
Absolutely! I work with clients from over 20 countries. All sessions are conducted in English, and I understand the unique challenges expats face living in Bangkok.
Can you help with nutrition while eating Thai food?
Yes! I help clients navigate Bangkok's amazing food scene healthily. We create sustainable meal plans using local ingredients and teach you how to make smart choices at Thai restaurants.
What are your rates for personal training in Bangkok?
Rates vary by location and package. Contact me for current pricing. I offer single sessions, monthly packages, and corporate rates. First consultation is always free.
